About The Problem

Many fruits and food products are mixed with harmful chemicals to improve appearance, increase shelf life, and gain profit. These practices affect health and reduce food quality.

Food Adulteration

Mixing low-quality or harmful substances with food products to increase quantity or profit.

Chemical Ripening

Chemicals like calcium carbide are used to artificially ripen fruits quickly.

Health Risks

Harmful chemicals may cause stomach pain, allergies, food poisoning, and long-term diseases.

Impact on Health

Safety Tips

Follow these simple methods to reduce the risk of consuming chemically treated fruits.

  • Wash fruits thoroughly before eating.
  • Avoid very shiny and unnaturally colored fruits.
  • Use salt water or vinegar water to clean fruits.
  • Buy fruits from trusted local sellers or farmers.
  • Prefer naturally ripened fruits whenever possible.
